Welcome

teoremer.com is collection of mathematical definitions, theorems, proofs and illustrations. All content is contributed by users. Read more.

Where to go from here?

Look around and browse and search for the definitions, theorems, proofs and illustrations that are on this site.

We very much encourage users to create new content: definitions, theorems, proofs and illustrations, to add validations and to vote on other user's contributions. The joint effort of many users is what makes this site useful.

For this you need an account, though. The site is currently in a closed beta state, meaning that new accounts are created by invitation only. Read more on the blog.
